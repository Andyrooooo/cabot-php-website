Biography:

I decided to pursue a career in the travel industry because I knew it was my passion from an early age. I was born in Germany, grew up in Greece, and took French during my school years. Growing up in Europe, where countries are as close to one another as states are in the US, I experienced different cultures throughout my formative years. I visited almost every country in Europe and traveled to many destinations in the Caribbean, Asia, Africa, and the Middle East. My favorite part about being a travel consultant is receiving the positive feedback from my clients upon their return. The most memorable vacations I've taken are climbing the Great Wall of China, visiting Amsterdam on a canal cruise while sailing the Rhine, and ziplining in Mexico. I definitely prefer a beach vacation, easily followed by discovering exotic destinations in Asia and Europe.
